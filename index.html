Who We Are
A consulting practice built at the intersection of business strategy and human capital.

Esscube Strategy Consultants is a specialist advisory firm working exclusively in the people and culture domain. We bring analytical rigour and strategic depth to the challenges that matter most — how organisations attract, compensate, develop, and retain the talent that drives their competitive advantage.

Headquartered in Bengaluru with presence in Mumbai, we work with boards, CHROs, and leadership teams across industries. Our approach is rooted in data, shaped by research, and designed for measurable impact.

Our dedicated division, Trusted Workplace, operates as a specialised practice focused on workplace culture assessment, people risk management, and organisational certification — helping companies build workplaces where people genuinely thrive.

Capabilities
The full spectrum of
people strategy advisory.
From the boardroom to the front line, we bring deep expertise across every dimension of human capital management.

Salary Benchmarking Studies

Comprehensive market compensation analysis across roles, levels, and industries. We design custom benchmarking frameworks that map your pay philosophy to real market data — covering base pay, variable compensation, benefits, and total rewards positioning.

CEO Compensation & Board Advisory

Independent advisory to boards and nomination & remuneration committees on executive compensation design, CEO performance frameworks, pay-for-performance alignment, and regulatory compliance under Companies Act and SEBI guidelines.

Performance Management Systems

Design and implementation of performance management architectures — from goal-setting frameworks and competency models to review processes and calibration mechanisms. We build systems that connect individual contribution to organisational strategy.

Retention Strategy & Planning

Data-driven retention diagnostics that identify flight risk, attrition drivers, and critical talent segments. We develop targeted retention plans combining career architecture, compensation levers, engagement interventions, and stay incentive structures.

ESOP Programme Design

End-to-end design of employee stock option programmes — from scheme architecture and vesting schedules to valuation methodology, tax planning, and board-ready documentation. Tailored for startups through listed enterprises.

Reward & Recognition Programmes

Strategic design of recognition ecosystems that reinforce culture, drive behaviours, and improve engagement. We build frameworks spanning peer recognition, milestone rewards, performance bonuses, and long-term incentive plans aligned to business cycles.

HR Policy Development

Comprehensive policy frameworks covering the entire employee lifecycle — from hiring and onboarding through conduct, leave, flexible working, grievance management, and separation. Designed for legal compliance, operational clarity, and cultural alignment.

Board-Level Recruitment

Executive search and selection advisory for board and C-suite appointments. We bring structured assessment methodologies, competency mapping, market intelligence, and governance best practices to leadership hiring at the highest levels.

Training & Development

Capability building programmes designed around business outcomes. We develop training architectures, identify skill gaps through competency assessments, design learning pathways, and create leadership development frameworks that accelerate talent readiness.

Workplace Culture Assessment

Through our Trusted Workplace division, we offer rigorous workplace culture diagnostics, people risk assessments, and certification programmes that help organisations measure, benchmark, and transform their employee experience.
